Aksharasamudram

"Aksharasamudram" program make a pathway to the coastal community in the field of education. In the first consignment, Aksharasamudram is the educational development program mainly for the school student, who appearing the 10th and 12th exam. Under the project students get free coaching classes and financial aid for the studies.

What Are We

The coastal areas of Calicut really did not get as much as development that the counter parts achieve. They are backward in the case of education, so people in these areas are not capable of enjoying the merits of modern society. To study the reasons of this backwardness, YES India, with the support of CIGI, conducted a survey among people of costal belt near Mukhadar and following were the major findings. It is unbelievable that still some boy-students drop their education before completing schooling. The reason is that they go for fishing from their early age itself. In the case of girls the studies terminates with marriage normally from the early part of their teen age. Parents are not bothered about need for education and students are not aware on plenty of career opportunities. All these lead to low performance of the students in their academics.


Basically the fishing community is a marginalized and neglected group of people because of their poor social economic status in the society and this community is a distinctive group of people geographically located in the coastal area and have their own way of life and distinctive culture. Education is the one of the key indicator of the expansion. In seaside people they may have their own identity and living style. So they not bothered about the education and particularly in coastal area in Kozhikode. Most of the communities are uneducated and high rate of drop out among the pupil. The real reason for the high rate of drop out in schooling is interlinked with different socio economic aspects of the society.



In this situation Aksharasamudram is the educational development program mainly for the school student, who appearing the 10th and 12th exam. Under the project students get free coaching classes and financial aid for the studies. The coastal population following the traditional occupation and the educational unawareness among them is high and every year 30% of the student dropout in seaside area. The project also make educational awareness classes for parents. The financial assistance and free classes attract the Students to enrolling themselves in the school and the ones who have already dropped out rejoined the school. This project is therefore a huge success in Kozhikode distric
